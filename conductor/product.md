# Product Guide

## Vision
To provide a secure, user-friendly, and privacy-focused macOS application for watermarking sensitive documents (like IDs and passports) before they are shared online. The app ensures that watermarks are either easy to add (Vector Mode) or impossible to remove (Secure Mode), while also stripping all EXIF metadata for maximum privacy.

## Target Audience
- Individuals who need to share digital copies of personal identification (passports, driver's licenses, etc.) for online applications or verification.
- Privacy-conscious users who want to ensure their metadata is not leaked when sharing files.

## Core Features
- **Customizable Watermarking**: Flexible control over text, opacity, size, spacing, color, and orientation.
- **Dynamic Export Formats**: Support for images (JPG, PNG) and PDF documents.
- **Secure Mode (Raster)**: Converts PDF pages into high-resolution images to flatten watermarks, making them inseparable from the content and preventing text extraction.
- **EXIF Metadata Stripping**: Automatically removes sensitive metadata from all exported files.
- **Real-time Preview**: Immediate visual feedback for all watermark adjustments.

## Success Criteria
- Users can quickly and easily watermark their sensitive documents.
- Exported files are stripped of metadata and contain correctly applied watermarks.
- Secure Mode produces high-quality, non-removable watermarks.
